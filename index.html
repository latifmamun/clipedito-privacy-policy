Privacy Policy for ClipEdito

Effective Date: July 31, 2025

ClipEdito ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ClipEdito (the "App"). The App is designed to provide video and audio editing tools, including trimming, merging, effects application, and conversion features.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Media Files: When you select videos, audio, or images within the App for editing, we access these files locally on your device. We do not upload or store these files on our servers unless explicitly required for app functionality (e.g., temporary caching for processing). All editing occurs on-device.
  • No Personal Account Information: The App does not require user registration, login, or collection of personal details such as name, email, or phone number.

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device and Usage Data: We may collect information about your device and how you interact with the App, including device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, IP address, mobile network information, app usage data, and crash reports (via Google Play Services).
  • Advertising Data: The App integrates Google AdMob for displaying ads. AdMob may collect data for personalized advertising, including advertising IDs (e.g., Android Advertising ID), device information, and approximate location (derived from IP address).

c. Permissions

The App requests permissions to access your device's storage, media files, and internet. These are used solely for reading/writing media files for editing and saving outputs, and for internet access to load ads (via AdMob).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To process and edit your media files locally, apply effects, and save outputs.
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage data to enhance performance, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • Advertising: Display relevant ads through AdMob. We do not sell your data but share limited device information with Google for ad personalization.
  • Compliance and Security: Ensure compliance with legal requirements and protect against misuse.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: Data is shared with Google (for AdMob ads and analytics) per their Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, such as in response to a subpoena or court order.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

4. Data Security

We prioritize security. All media processing occurs on-device, and we do not store files remotely. However, no method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may use cookies or similar technologies for personalized ads. Opt out via device settings or Google account preferences.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 and will delete it if discovered.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Control: Manage permissions in device settings.
  • Opt-Out of Ads: Disable personalized ads in Google account or device settings.
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the App removes all local data.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy, with the updated "Effective Date" noted. Review periodically; continued use after changes indicates acceptance.
